Akhal-Teke horse

Scientists claim that due to the horses’ genetics, their amazing coats reflect and retract light, just as metal would.

  • Scientific Name: Equus ferus caballus
  • Average Weight: 450 kg
  • Habitat: Turkmenistan, Uzbekistan, Kazakhstan, Russia
  • Personality: incredibly athletic breed

With only 1,250 of these horses in existence, it’s little wonder that the Chinese refer to them as “horses from heaven”. They have a great streak of surviving in the cold and extreme climates and have lightning speed, intelligence, and endurance.
